16/01/2015 My First Post

Assalamualaikum
Posted By Hasu, January 16, 2015, in Perfume

Hi guys. I wanted to build a website for quite some time already and finally, here it is! I was so excited that I took the whole day tweaking here and there.

Just a little dab and I can still smell it. Long-lasting is a sign of authenticity.

Today I’ve just received some perfume samples from vendor. I can’t wait to do some comparison/review on their product. Let’s hope we are on the right track.

I might not be able to make a post in these samples all at once but I’ll try. In fact, I’ve tested one already, just a little dab and I can still smell it. Long-lasting is a sign of authenticity.

Once I finished evaluate/testing the products, I’ll be officially taking orders. It’s going to be fun.

Here on this blog side of the website, I’ll post useful information/tips on perfume. So, stay tune. XOXO
